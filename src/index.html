Welcome to
City States Medieval

CS:M Is a cross platform empire building mmo currently in development for mobile(tablet-smartphone) and desktop computers.

While similar to other games on first sight, CS:M is different in may ways. Visually we decided to go for the full 3d experience in low poly style. Every building and icon has handpainted with love to the details.

Resources in the game are also issued as real world assets on the stellar network. Players trade resources on a global exchange with each other instead of the engine or prices made up by the developers.

Unique
PLAY 2 EARN CONCEPT

Probably the most enthralling part of our game concepts is the option for players to earn money while playing the game.

The global market is balanced by real supply and demand of all players not some made up algorithm. Changing the output of your city state by gathering crafting resources that are in high demand or simply trading smart can yield profit on the marketplace.

Profits can then turned into actual currency and requested to be paid out.

Hex
OUR IN GAME CURRENCY

Every trade, buy and sell in CS:M is using HEX. Hex is a token on the stellar network that can be bought with laps or gathered by producing and trading smart.

We have several competitions and other features planned where HEX can be won or obtained without the need to pay for.

Our goal is to have a competitive balance between Play 2 Earn and paying players. One should not be able to pay their way out!
